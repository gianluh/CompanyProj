Our Available Products

EcoCharge SolarPack

A backpack with integrated solar panels and a high-capacity battery to charge electronic devices anywhere. Perfect for hikers, travelers, and mobile workers.

Features:

  • Foldable high-efficiency solar panels
  • 20,000 mAh power bank with fast charging
  • USB-C and USB-A ports for multiple devices
  • Water and shock-resistant design

EcoCharge HomeStation

A home charging station with solar panels and an intelligent storage system, ideal for reducing dependence on traditional
power grids.

Features:

  • Expandable 5 kWh lithium-ion modular battery
  • Smart inverter to optimize energy consumption
  • Monitoring app to track production and consumption
  • Compatible with existing home solar systems

EcoCharge Portable

A portable solar-powered charger with fast-charging technology, ideal for emergencies and travel.

Features:

  • 10,000 mAh capacity with integrated solar panel
  • LED flashlight with SOS mode
  • Compact and water-resistant design
  • Suitable for smartphones, tablets, and small electronics

EcoCharge UltraGrid

An off-grid power system for homes and businesses in
remote areas, based on solar and wind energy.

Features:

  • Combination of solar and wind energy for maximum efficiency
  • Storage capacity of up to 20 kWh
  • Backup system to ensure power even at night
  • Perfect for isolated homes, cabins, and off-grid establishments

EcoCharge AutoDock

A solar-powered electric vehicle charging station, designed for homes and businesses looking to reduce their environmental
impact.

Features:

  • Integrated solar panels for eco-friendly charging
  • Compatible with all standard electric vehicles
  • Easy installation and scalability
  • Energy monitoring via mobile app

EcoCharge SmartLamp

A solar-powered smart lamp that provides eco-friendly
lighting for both indoor and outdoor spaces. Perfect for patios, gardens, or as a sustainable lighting solution for homes.

Features:

  • Adjustable brightness with smart controls (via app or voice assistant)
  • Integrated solar panel for wireless charging during the day
  • Motion sensor for automatic on/off functionality
  • IP65 waterproof and durable design for all weather conditions
